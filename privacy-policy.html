1 Information We Collect

When you interact with Procure Lead, we may collect certain personally identifiable information ("Personal Information"). This includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Job title

Any additional information you voluntarily provide while interacting with our services.

We may also collect non-personal information, such as IP addresses and browsing behavior, through cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience.

2 Use of Personal Information

We may use your Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide and improve our lead procurement services as requested by you.
  • Communication: To send you updates, newsletters, marketing materials, and other relevant communications. You can opt out of these communications at any time.
  • Website optimization: To analyze site performance, usage trends, and improve functionality.
  • Legal compliance: To fulfill any legal obligations or respond to legal requests.
3 Sharing of Personal Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell or rent your Personal Information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following scenarios:

  • Service providers: With trusted third-party partners who assist in providing our services, under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal obligations: When required by law or in response to legal requests.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your Personal Information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.
4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can control your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
